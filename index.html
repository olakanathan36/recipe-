Grandma's Secret Apple pie

This apple pie recipe has been in my family for generations and never fails to delight

Ingredient of Apple Pie

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp gound cinnamon
  • 6 apples,peeled and sliced
    • Instruction

  • Preheat over to 375~400 deg
  • In the bowl, fix the apple and chinnamon
  • Toss the apple in sugar-chinnamon mixture
